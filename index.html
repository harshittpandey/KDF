About Us
  • Football is a passion for thousands of Indians. We are the NGO, registered under Indian society act 1956. We are operating Youth Soccer Training Program under Youth Empowerment Project. We are working with 200+ Youth and we have various scholarship programs for Underprivileged Youth of our Society for Sports and Education.

  • Basic aimed at producing future stars for soccer excellence who will carry the baton of promoting the attitude and uplifting Indian Soccer in front of the world. Targeted at producing football talents who will make the country proud in international tournaments, a part of our aim is to build an Indian Dream with a vision to qualify for the main round of the 2022 World Cup and thereafter. The Vision 2022, is a small step to achieve the goal of witnessing the birth of a new soccer nation that can hold its head with pride and dignity in front of International audience. This academy, however, based on an official understanding on 14th Feb 2014.
